Eligibility Criteria

As a matter of safety, to be eligible for our programs each participant must be able to:

  • Tread water for 2 minutes.
  • Swim 50 yards (fully clothed).
  • Put on a life jacket while in the water.

Our programs are open to everyone. Please email us or call 866-555-1212 if you would like to participate but require special accomodations.

What You'll Need

Each participant will need to be sure to have the following items:

  • A well fitted wetsuit or drysuit.
  • A USCG approved, Type III life jacket.
  • A whistle securely attached to life jacket.

Don't have a life jacket or wetsuit? Please email us or call 866-555-1212 if we can assist you with getting these items together.

Cancellation Policy

We'll need at least 14 days cancellation notice prior to your scheduled program, or we cannot provide a refund. Each program requires a minimum of 3 participants. We'll notify you at least 14 days prior if your scheduled program is canceled.

Scholarships Available

We pride ourselves on making our programs accessible to our community. Scholarships are available to help with the costs of program fees, equipment rentals, and snacks & lunches. Please email us or call 866-555-1212 for more information.

Forms

Each participant will need to sign a liability waiver form. You can fill out the form online.

What's Next?

The next step in our kayak program is our Advanced Kayak Camp, which includes an ACA skills assessment. Once completed, participants will be qualified for employment as kayak guides.
